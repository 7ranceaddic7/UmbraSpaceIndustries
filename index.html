Welcome to the Umbra Space Industries!

USI publishes a wide variety of mods for Kerbal Space Program, including mods for colonization, resource gathering, and various part packs. You can browse our offerings below. Included are links to the current release as well as the relevant KSP forum thread. Enjoy!

Want to keep up with the latest version? All USI mods use KSP-AVC and check directly against the GitHub repo!

Like the mods and want to show your support (or just buy me a cup of coffee)? Click on the Patreon link below!
